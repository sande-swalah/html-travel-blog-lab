Adventure in Fire Island

Fire island is a tropical island located in the coast of Kenya. It is filled with numerous activities that include beautiful sunsets, renowned restaurants as well as a pristine beach along the coastline. It is truly a place designed for relaxation and leisure. If you need a place to visit in your vacation visit Fire Island!
